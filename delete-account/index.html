Delete Account

This page explains how users of Minimal Journal can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please follow the steps below to delete your account:

  1. Send an email to mohammedasif.dev@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Minimal Journal Account
  3. Include the email address used to sign in to the app

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your request is processed, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your Minimal Journal user account
  • Email address associated with the account
  • All journal entries and saved content

Data Retention

After account deletion, your personal data is removed from active systems. Any remaining backup data is automatically deleted within 30 days.
