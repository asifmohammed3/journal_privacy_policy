Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 January 2026

Antigravity Journal ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Unique user ID generated during authentication

b) User Content

  • Journal entries created by you inside the app

We do not collect phone numbers, contacts, location data, payment information, or device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Store and sync journal entries
  • Provide a personalized and secure app experience

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Authentication & Backend Services

Antigravity Journal uses Supabase as its backend service for secure authentication and encrypted data storage.

Google Sign-In is used only to verify your identity. We do not access or store your Google password or other private Google account information.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • User data is securely stored on Supabase servers
  • Industry-standard security practices are used to protect your data

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data is only processed by trusted infrastructure providers necessary for operating the app (such as Supabase).

6. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To request data deletion, please contact us using the email address below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Antigravity Journal is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
